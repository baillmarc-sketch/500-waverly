Furniture

Tap a piece to drop it on the Main Level. Drag to position, then use the toolbar to rotate or delete.

How it works

A quick tour — everything is to scale and auto-saves on this device.

🪑 Arrange

  • Drag any piece to move it (it snaps to walls and lines up with other pieces).
  • Double-tap a piece — or grab the round handle — to rotate it.
  • Select a piece for the toolbar: rotate, recolor 🎨, duplicate, bring to front, delete.
  • Made a mistake? Undo (Ctrl/⌘ Z, the toast button, or ⋯ → Undo).

➕ Add & switch

  • ＋ Add furniture opens the catalog — tap a piece to drop it in.
  • Toggle Main / Roof at the top to switch floors.
  • ⌂ Layouts loads designer templates; ⟲ Reset restores the Feng Shui layout.

🔎 View & tools

  • Pinch (or scroll) to zoom, drag the background to pan, ⤢ to re-fit.
  • 📏 Measure: drag across the plan to read a distance.
  • 💡 Ideas has decorator & Feng Shui notes.

💾 Saving

  • Your layout auto-saves in this browser — reopen the page and it's still here.
  • 🔗 Share saves it online as a link (no account, no extra apps). After you tap Share, the page address tracks your edits — bookmark the page (or Add to Home Screen) and that bookmark restores this exact layout on any device.
  • Send the link to anyone and it opens their copy to edit. ↧ Export saves a file backup you can re-import.
